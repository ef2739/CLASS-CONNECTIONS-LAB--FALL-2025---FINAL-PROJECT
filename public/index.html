What is violence?
Violence against women and girls (VAW123) is any form of violence disproportionately perpetrated by men against women and girls.
It is deeply linked to women’s inequality and includes sexual violence, domestic abuse, stalking and harassment, female genital mutilation, forced marriage and so-called ‘honour-based’ abuse, exploitation and abuse of women and girls in online spaces. //https://www.endviolenceagainstwomen.org.uk/explainer///
